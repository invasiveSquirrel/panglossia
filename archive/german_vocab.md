# German Vocabulary

## Essential Verbs
- **sein** (to be)
  - IPA: [zaɪ̯n]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: bin, bist, ist (present); war, warst, war (past); gewesen (past participle)
  - Example: "Ich bin ein Lehrer" (I am a teacher)

- **haben** (to have)
  - IPA: [ˈhaːbn̩]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: habe, hast, hat (present); hatte, hattest, hatte (past); gehabt (past participle)
  - Example: "Ich habe ein Buch" (I have a book)

- **machen** (to do/make)
  - IPA: [ˈmaxn̩]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: mache, machst, macht (present); machte, machtest, machte (past); gemacht (past participle)
  - Example: "Was machst du?" (What do you do?)

- **gehen** (to go)
  - IPA: [ˈɡeːən]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: gehe, gehst, geht (present); ging, gingst, ging (past); gegangen (past participle)
  - Example: "Ich gehe zum Markt" (I go to the market)

- **kommen** (to come)
  - IPA: [ˈkɔmən]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: komme, kommst, kommt (present); kam, kamst, kam (past); gekommen (past participle)
  - Example: "Er kommt morgen" (He comes tomorrow)

- **verstehen** (to understand, separable prefix: ver- [inseparable])
  - IPA: [fɛɐ̯ˈʃteːən]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb (inseparable prefix)
  - Conjugations: verstehe, verstehst, versteht (present); verstand, verstanden, verstanden (past); verstanden (past participle)
  - Example: "Ich verstehe Deutsch" (I understand German)

- **ankommen** (to arrive, separable prefix: an- [separable])
  - IPA: [ˈɑŋkɔmən]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb (separable prefix)
  - Conjugations: komme an, kommst an, kommt an (present); kam an, kamst an, kam an (past); angekommen (past participle)
  - Example: "Der Zug kommt um 10 Uhr an" (The train arrives at 10 o'clock)

- **anrufen** (to call/phone, separable prefix: an- [separable])
  - IPA: [ˈɑnruːfən]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb (separable prefix)
  - Conjugations: rufe an, rufst an, ruft an (present); rief an, riefst an, rief an (past); angerufen (past participle)
  - Example: "Ich rufe dich an" (I call you)

## Common Nouns
- **Mann** (man)
  - IPA: [man]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Der Mann ist intelligent" (The man is intelligent)

- **Frau** (woman)
  - IPA: [fɾaʊ̯]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Die Frau arbeitet hier" (The woman works here)

- **Kind** (child)
  - IPA: [kɪnt]
  - Gender: neuter
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Das Kind spielt im Park" (The child plays in the park)

- **Wasser** (water)
  - IPA: [ˈvasɐ]
  - Gender: neuter
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Ich muss Wasser trinken" (I must drink water)

- **Brot** (bread)
  - IPA: [bɾoːt]
  - Gender: neuter
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Ich kaufte Brot im Laden" (I bought bread in the store)

- **Haus** (house)
  - IPA: [haʊ̯s]
  - Gender: neuter
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Mein Haus ist groß" (My house is big)

- **Tag** (day)
  - IPA: [taːk]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Heute ist ein schöner Tag" (Today is a beautiful day)

- **Nacht** (night)
  - IPA: [naxt]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Ich schlafe nachts gut" (I sleep well at night)

## Basic Adjectives
- **groß** (big)
  - IPA: [ɡɾoːs]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Ein großes Auto" (A big car)

- **klein** (small)
  - IPA: [klaɪ̯n]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Ein kleines Haus" (A small house)

- **gut** (good)
  - IPA: [ɡuːt]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Das ist eine gute Idee" (That's a good idea)

- **schlecht** (bad)
  - IPA: [ʃlɛxt]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Das ist schlechtes Verhalten" (That's bad behavior)

- **neu** (new)
  - IPA: [nɔɪ̯]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Ich kaufte ein neues Auto" (I bought a new car)

- **alt** (old)
  - IPA: [alt]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Dieses Haus ist sehr alt" (This house is very old)

## Common Phrases
- **Hallo** (hello)
  - IPA: [ˈhalo]
  - Part of Speech: Interjection
  - Example: "Hallo! Wie geht es dir?" (Hello! How are you?)

- **Danke** (thank you)
  - IPA: [ˈdaŋkə]
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Vielen Dank für deine Hilfe" (Thank you very much for your help)

- **Bitte** (please/you're welcome)
  - IPA: [ˈbɪtə]
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Ein Kaffee, bitte" (A coffee, please)

- **Auf Wiedersehen** (goodbye)
  - IPA: [aʊ̯f ˈviːdɐzeːən]
  - Part of Speech: Interjection
  - Example: "Auf Wiedersehen! Bis später" (Goodbye! See you later)
