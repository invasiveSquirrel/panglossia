# Finnish Vocabulary

## Essential Verbs
- **olla** (to be)
  - IPA: [ˈollɑ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: olen, olit, oli (past); ollut (past participle)
  - Example: "Olen opettaja" (I am a teacher)

- **tehdä** (to do/make)
  - IPA: [ˈtehdæ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: tein, teit, teki (past); tehnyt (past participle) [stem change: teke- → te-]
  - Example: "Mitä teet?" (What do you do?)

- **mennä** (to go)
  - IPA: [ˈmenːæ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: menin, menit, meni (past); mennyt (past participle) [gemination]
  - Example: "Menen kauppaan" (I go to the market)

- **tulla** (to come)
  - IPA: [ˈtulːɑ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: tulin, tulit, tuli (past); tullut (past participle) [gemination]
  - Example: "Hän tulee huomenna" (He comes tomorrow)

- **antaa** (to give)
  - IPA: [ˈɑntɑː]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: annoin, annoit, antoi (past); antanut (past participle) [stem change: ant- → anno-]
  - Example: "Annan sinulle kirjan" (I give you a book)

## Basic Adjectives
- **iso** (big)
  - IPA: [ˈiso]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Iso auto" (A big car)

- **pieni** (small)
  - IPA: [ˈpieni]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Pieni talo" (A small house)

- **hyvä** (good)
  - IPA: [ˈhyvæ]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Se on hyvä idea" (That's a good idea)

- **huono** (bad)
  - IPA: [ˈhuono]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Se on huono käytös" (That's bad behavior)

- **uusi** (new)
  - IPA: [ˈuusi]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Ostin uuden auton" (I bought a new car)

- **vanha** (old)
  - IPA: [ˈvɑnhɑ]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Tämä talo on hyvin vanha" (This house is very old)

## Common Phrases
- **Hei** (hello)
  - IPA: [ˈhei]
  - Part of Speech: Interjection
  - Example: "Hei! Kuinka voit?" (Hello! How are you?)

- **Kiitos** (thank you)
  - IPA: [ˈkiitos]
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Suuri kiitos avustastasi" (Thank you very much for your help)

- **Ystävä** (friend)
  - IPA: [ˈystævæ]
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Hän on minun ystävä" (He is my friend)

- **Ole hyvä** (please/you're welcome)
  - IPA: [ˈole hyvæ]
  - Part of Speech: Phrase
  - Example: "Kahvi, ole hyvä" (Coffee, please)

- **Arvoisa** (respected/Dear)
  - IPA: [ˈɑrvoisɑ]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Arvoisa ystävä" (Dear friend)
