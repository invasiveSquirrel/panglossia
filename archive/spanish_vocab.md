# Spanish Vocabulary

## Essential Verbs (Most Common)
- **ser** (to be - permanent)
  - IPA: [seɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: soy, eres, es (present); fui, fuiste, fue (past); sido (past participle)
  - Example: "Yo soy ingeniero" (I am an engineer)

- **estar** (to be - location/state)
  - IPA: [esˈtaɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: estoy, estás, está (present); estuve, estuviste, estuvo (past); estado (past participle)
  - Example: "Estoy en la casa" (I am in the house)

- **tener** (to have)
  - IPA: [teˈneɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: tengo, tienes, tiene (present); tuve, tuviste, tuvo (past); tenido (past participle)
  - Example: "Tengo un libro" (I have a book)

- **hacer** (to do/make)
  - IPA: [aˈθeɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: hago, haces, hace (present); hice, hiciste, hizo (past); hecho (past participle)
  - Example: "¿Qué haces?" (What do you do?)

- **ir** (to go)
  - IPA: [iɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: voy, vas, va (present); fui, fuiste, fue (past); ido (past participle)
  - Example: "Voy al mercado" (I go to the market)

## Common Nouns
- **hombre** (man)
  - IPA: [ˈombɾe]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "El hombre es inteligente" (The man is intelligent)

- **mujer** (woman)
  - IPA: [muˈxeɾ]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "La mujer trabaja aquí" (The woman works here)

- **niño** (child/boy)
  - IPA: [ˈniɲo]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "El niño juega en el parque" (The child plays in the park)

- **agua** (water)
  - IPA: [ˈaɣwa]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Necesito beber agua" (I need to drink water)

- **pan** (bread)
  - IPA: [pan]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Compré pan en la tienda" (I bought bread in the store)

- **casa** (house)
  - IPA: [ˈkasa]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Mi casa es grande" (My house is big)

- **día** (day)
  - IPA: [ˈdia]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Hoy es un buen día" (Today is a good day)

- **noche** (night)
  - IPA: [ˈnotʃe]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Duermo bien por la noche" (I sleep well at night)

## Basic Adjectives
- **grande** (big)
  - IPA: [ɡɾanˈde]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Un coche grande" (A big car)

- **pequeño** (small)
  - IPA: [peˈkeɲo]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Una casa pequeña" (A small house)

- **bueno** (good)
  - IPA: [ˈbweno]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Es una buena idea" (It's a good idea)

- **malo** (bad)
  - IPA: [ˈmalo]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Ese es un mal comportamiento" (That's bad behavior)

- **nuevo** (new)
  - IPA: [ˈnwebo]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Compré un coche nuevo" (I bought a new car)

- **viejo** (old)
  - IPA: [ˈbjexo]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Esta casa es muy vieja" (This house is very old)

## Common Phrases & Words
- **hola** (hello)
  - IPA: [ˈola]
  - Part of Speech: Interjection
  - Example: "¡Hola! ¿Cómo estás?" (Hello! How are you?)

- **gracias** (thank you)
  - IPA: [ˈɡɾasjas]
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Muchas gracias por tu ayuda" (Thank you very much for your help)

- **por favor** (please)
  - IPA: [poɾ faˈβoɾ]
  - Part of Speech: Adverbial phrase
  - Example: "Un café, por favor" (A coffee, please)

- **de nada** (you're welcome)
  - IPA: [de ˈnada]
  - Part of Speech: Adverbial phrase
  - Example: "De nada, siempre feliz de ayudar" (You're welcome, always happy to help)

- **adiós** (goodbye)
  - IPA: [aˈðjos]
  - Part of Speech: Interjection
  - Example: "¡Adiós! Hasta luego" (Goodbye! See you later)
