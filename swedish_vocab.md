# Swedish Vocabulary

## Colors
- **Röd** (Red)
  - IPA: [rœd]
  - Gender: Common
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Rosen är röd" (The rose is red)

## Animals  
- **Hund** (Dog)
  - IPA: [hɵnd]
  - Gender: Common (en hund)
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Hunden springer" (The dog runs)

- **Katt** (Cat)
  - IPA: [katt]
  - Gender: Common (en katt)
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Katten sover" (The cat sleeps)

## Food
- **Bröd** (Bread)
  - IPA: [brœd]
  - Gender: Neuter (ett bröd)
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Jag äter bröd" (I eat bread)

- **Mjölk** (Milk)
  - IPA: [mjœlk]
  - Gender: Common (en mjölk)
  - Tone: 2
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "En glas mjölk" (A glass of milk)

## Basic Verbs
- **Gå** (To go)
  - IPA: [ɡoː]
  - Tone: 2
  - Part of Speech: Verb (infinitive)
  - Conjugations: går (present), gick (past), gått (past participle)
  - Example: "Jag går till skolan" (I go to school)

- **Äta** (To eat)
  - IPA: [ˈɛːta]
  - Tone: 2
  - Part of Speech: Verb (infinitive)
  - Conjugations: äter (present), åt (past), ätit (past participle)
  - Example: "Vi äter middag" (We eat dinner)
