# Portuguese Vocabulary

## Essential Verbs
- **ser** (to be - permanent)
  - IPA: [seɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: sou (I am), és (you are), é (he/she is)
  - Example: "Eu sou engenheiro" (I am an engineer)

- **estar** (to be - location/state)
  - IPA: [ɨʃˈtaɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: estou (I am), estás (you are), está (he/she is)
  - Example: "Estou em casa" (I am at home)

- **ter** (to have)
  - IPA: [teɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: tenho (I have), tens (you have), tem (he/she has)
  - Example: "Tenho um livro" (I have a book)

- **fazer** (to do/make)
  - IPA: [fɐˈzeɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: faço (I do), fazes (you do), faz (he/she does)
  - Example: "O que fazes?" (What do you do?)

- **ir** (to go)
  - IPA: [iɾ]
  - Gender: N/A
  - Tone: 1
  - Part of Speech: Verb
  - Conjugations: vou (I go), vais (you go), vai (he/she goes)
  - Example: "Vou ao mercado" (I go to the market)

## Common Nouns
- **homem** (man)
  - IPA: [oˈmɛ̃]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "O homem é inteligente" (The man is intelligent)

- **mulher** (woman)
  - IPA: [muˈʎeɾ]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "A mulher trabalha aqui" (The woman works here)

- **criança** (child)
  - IPA: [kɾiˈɐ̃sɐ]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "A criança brinca no parque" (The child plays in the park)

- **água** (water)
  - IPA: [ˈaɡwɐ]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Preciso beber água" (I need to drink water)

- **pão** (bread)
  - IPA: [pɐ̃w]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Comprei pão na loja" (I bought bread in the store)

- **casa** (house)
  - IPA: [ˈkazɐ]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Minha casa é grande" (My house is big)

- **dia** (day)
  - IPA: [ˈdiɐ]
  - Gender: masculine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Hoje é um bom dia" (Today is a good day)

- **noite** (night)
  - IPA: [ˈnɔjtɨ]
  - Gender: feminine
  - Part of Speech: Noun
  - Example: "Durmo bem à noite" (I sleep well at night)

## Basic Adjectives
- **grande** (big)
  - IPA: [ɡɾɐ̃dɨ]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Um carro grande" (A big car)

- **pequeno** (small)
  - IPA: [pɨˈkenu]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Uma casa pequena" (A small house)

- **bom** (good)
  - IPA: [bõ]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "É uma boa ideia" (It's a good idea)

- **mau** (bad)
  - IPA: [maw]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Esse é um mau comportamento" (That's bad behavior)

- **novo** (new)
  - IPA: [ˈnovu]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Comprei um carro novo" (I bought a new car)

- **velho** (old)
  - IPA: [ˈveʎu]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective
  - Example: "Esta casa é muito velha" (This house is very old)

## Common Phrases
- **Olá** (hello)
  - IPA: [oˈla]
  - Part of Speech: Interjection
  - Example: "Olá! Como estás?" (Hello! How are you?)

- **Obrigado** (thank you)
  - IPA: [oβɾiˈɡadu]
  - Part of Speech: Adjective/Interjection
  - Example: "Muito obrigado pela tua ajuda" (Thank you very much for your help)

- **Por favor** (please)
  - IPA: [puɾ fɐˈvoɾ]
  - Part of Speech: Adverbial phrase
  - Example: "Um café, por favor" (A coffee, please)

- **De nada** (you're welcome)
  - IPA: [dɨ ˈnadɐ]
  - Part of Speech: Adverbial phrase
  - Example: "De nada, sempre feliz em ajudar" (You're welcome, always happy to help)

- **Adeus** (goodbye)
  - IPA: [ɐˈdew]
  - Part of Speech: Interjection
  - Example: "Adeus! Até logo" (Goodbye! See you later)
